What I Do

Electrical Services for
Homes & Small Businesses

Whether it's a simple repair or a full rewire, I handle it all personally — with the skill and care that comes from over a decade in the trade.

Electrical Repairs

Outlets not working? Breakers tripping? Flickering lights? I diagnose and fix electrical problems quickly and correctly.

Panel Upgrades

Upgrade your outdated electrical panel to safely support modern appliances and EV chargers. Code-compliant and fully permitted.

Lighting Installation

Recessed lighting, ceiling fans, outdoor fixtures, and LED retrofits. I make sure everything is perfectly placed and safely wired.

Wiring & Rewiring

New construction wiring or updating knob-and-tube in older homes — done cleanly, to code, with minimal disruption to your walls.

Troubleshooting

Mysterious electrical gremlins are my specialty. I track down the root cause — not just the symptom — so the problem stays fixed.

Emergency Services

Electrical emergencies don't wait. Call me any time — I offer after-hours service for urgent situations that can't wait until Monday.
